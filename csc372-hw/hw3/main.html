Restaurants

UNCG MEALS

Are you struggling to find good places to eat at around UNCG? If so, you are in the right place. This website will give you the best food suggestions around campus, whether it be a quick meal, or a dine in establishment. Maybe you are vegetarian or have other dietary restrictions, this website is also for you. Our top priority is to make sure you can find what you need during your time at UNCG!

Bojangles

Specialties

Fried chicken, cajun cuisine

Hours:

Monday-Friday: 5:30AM-10PM

Saturday: Closed

Sunday: 6AM-10PM

Address

1501 Spring Garden St, Greensboro, NC 27412

4pc Supreme Dinner

Price: $9.99

This meal is a staple lunch for me and friends on campus. It comes with 4 chicken tenders, a side of either dirty rice or french fries, and a drink of your choice. this meal is around 1000 calories and is great if you are looking for some fast food

Cajun Filet Biscuit

Price: $4.19

This biscuit is a great breakfast snack before class. It has around 570 calories, which is sure to get you through your morning classes

20 Piece Chicken Meal

Price: $51.99

This meal is a great option for a party or get together. With is calory count ranging anywhere from 9000 to 13000 calories, it will be enough to feed a small group of friends

The Don

Specialties

Ramen, Ishiyaki

Hours:

Monday-Friday: 11AM-3:30PM, 5PM-10PM

Saturday: 12PM-10PM

Sunday: Closed

Address

423 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403

Chicken Yakisoba

Price: $12.20

This meal is a great option at this local Japanese restaurant. The meal is very filling and is great after a long day of studying.

Miso Ramen

Price: $10.95

Miso ramen is a go to dish at the Don. The dish includes a variety of vegetables and noodles, as well as chicken for protein.

Spicy Ramen

Price: $10.95

This meal is a great option if the miso ramen felt like it was lacking a little kick. The dish is made complete with its chili broth and the beef protein.
